Alright Teams!

Preliminary games are set to begin Wednesday the 19th of October! According to feedback from the players we've spoken to already, 6:30 PM seems to work best for most players. If you find yourself scheduled on a night that that particular time doesn't work with you, please feel free to email and we'll do our best to adjust what we can. That being said, if an agreement cannot be reached between teams, the game will continue to be scheduled for 6:30 PM. Any team that doesn't show for the game will be disqualified.

Joining and playing the games

In the interest of ease of use, we'll be running the prelim games through the “Custom Game” option that already exists in League of Legends. Basically, we will email the captains of each team the name and password of the game on or before the day of the game. The captains will join the server and the game admin will then take a spectator spot in the match. Basically, they break down to:

  1. Be respectful. To your team and to the other team. Blatant disrespect will not be tolerated; i.e. swearing/cursing at your team or the opposing team, belittling or harassing, etc.
  2. No cheating. No third party apps or programs are to be used outside of Admin approved ones (i.e. Mumble servers for voice chat that we will be providing.).
  3. Have fun and make sure everyone else can too!
We want this to be an outstanding event and as successful as possible so that we are able to do it again. Let's keep gaming alive in northern Minnesota!

Lone Wolves

Your teams have been finalized and you should be receiving an email if you haven't already, letting you know what team you're on and who your team captain is. It's important that you guys and gals have some time to get together and start to gel as a team. To that end, the available byes for the first round of games have been given to you to give you a little extra time to finalize your line ups.

Team Captains

It will be your job to be on the front lines of communication between us and your team. If you have a player drop out of the tournament and need a replacement, it'll be on you to let us know. Outside of that, you in no way need to feel compelled to order your team around and call the shots. If that's what your team wants, awesome, but we're not trying to shovel any extra stress on you if we can help it.

A Paul Bunyan Admin will be available to spectate each game, so if you have questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to ask. We'll try to have the games up at least 15 min. before game time, so if everyone can get in and is ready to go sooner than 6:30pm, we can make that work too. Please feel free to email us at if you have any questions or concerns. Good luck and have fun!!!
