Hey Players!

We just wanted to send out an email to answer a couple of the most frequently asked questions we've been getting lately.

“You guys said prelims start on the Wednesday, the 19th of October.We can't play then/what time do we play/can we get a schedule/etc.”

So, we're going to START doing prelims on Wednesday, the 19th. There are 10 games in the first set, and we will have a PBC admin watching each one. That being said, we're planning on doing 2-3 games a night or so, depending on the availability of our admins. So, while the games are starting Wednesday, there may only be 4 teams playing that night. The other game nights will be Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays. Based on the feedback we've received so far, it sounds like 6:30pm works best for everyone. If you find yourself scheduled at a time that doesn't work for your team, let us know and we'll do our best to make it work for everyone. We're working on scheduling the games now and as soon as we know the schedule, you'll know the schedule. The first set of games will be between premade registrants only. We thought it would be best to give the Lone Wolf teams a little extra time to play together and gel as a team, so they will be playing in the second set of games, of which there will be approx. 5-6. After that set of games we'll have our final 8 that are going on to the "physical" games that will take place on the 12th of November. The prelim games will all take place online, so you can play from wherever you wish.

“I'm on a Lone Wolf team and can't get a hold of my captain/a player/various team mates.”

We're working on this. Most of you who have had problems have notified us, but if you haven't yet, let us know! We're working on filling spots and rounding out the teams as best we can. Hopefully we'll have all of these issues solved very shortly and you can get on with practicing. That shouldn't, however, stop you from practicing with the team mates that you are in contact with already. Get to know each other's strengths and weaknesses as best you can now, and let your fill ins know what you do when they finally touch base with you.
