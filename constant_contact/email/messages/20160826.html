Three weeks from League of Legends Registration Cutoff!

Hey Summoners, we wanted to remind you that the registration cut off for the Gigazone Gaming League of Legends tournament is September 16th. Even if you don't have a full team together you can register a captain and two other players. There are many lone wolf sign ups which will be used to fill in empty player spots.

There's no level requirement for the tournament, so whether your a level 1 summoner or a level 30 veteran we want you to participate!

After the registration cut off we'll be contacting the team captains to schedule preliminary matches to determine the final 8 teams that will compete at the event on November 12th.
