We have had several question about the League of Legends preliminary matches. Rather than replying to everyone individually the below are the most asked:

Q: When do the preliminary rounds start?

A: As soon as possible, when we know we'll send out messages to the team captains with that info.

Q: What does "single elimination to 6 teams, 2 teams high score wildcards." mean?

A: On gameday at the Sanford Center we will have time and resources for 8 teams to compete. The tricky part there is narrowing down the comptetion to just those 8 teams. To accomplish this, we will have all teams compete in a single elimination tournament to the point that we have it narrowed down to 6 teams. At that point we're going to take a look back at the 4 top scoring (by kill count) teams that lost. Those teams will then play one more game each (1 team vs. 1 team) and the 2 winning teams of those games will fill the final 2 "wildcard" slots. We will be posting a set of tournament rules very soon and the process should be explained in more detail at that time.

Q: Can my team play it's qualifying match at different time?

A: We will make efforts to find a time that will work for you to play in the preliminaries.

Q: I signed up as a lone wolf, when will I be put onto a team?

A: As soon as we know you will know.

If you have any other questions feel free to ask!

- Josh, Jay and Nate for Gigazone Gaming
